Ronaldo left Madrid in 2018 for the Italian giants Juventus for a transfer price of €100 million-plus €12 million in additional costs. Ronaldo signed a four-year deal with Juventus, and the fee paid by the new team was the most ever amount paid for any 30-year-old player. Ronaldo scored his first goal for Juventus against Sassuolo in his fourth game for the club, and he was sent off for the first time in his 154th Champions League matches against them.
